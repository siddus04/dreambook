Product Philosophy:

Refer to these points when thinking through features/ideas/experiences
- We want to build a product that is inspired by the fenyman technique and enables learning by doing/reinforcement: understand, explain, practise, build, teach, simplify & storytell, transfer (in no particular oder)
- We want to equip students for the AI age. Enable them to synthesize info fast but also develop critical thinking & first principles. Equip them to use AI and become a master at it. Develop taste and judgement.
-Reference the research and reference UX when brainstorming ideas

Student view:
- Student reads or listen the chapter to get the foundations. Its an enhanced experience. They can highlight, annottate text, and ask questions to their classmates/TA/Professor. They can ask the AI copilot to explain a difficult concept or brainstorm with the co pilot. They can explain their understanding and ask the AI to pretent like a student until they get it right. They can generate summaries, study guide/cheat sheet for exams, flashcards. They can ask for real world examples and see interesting snippets of the concept in the news, clearly marking out external sources. They have an MCP that enables them to bring their own resources or sync to their notes/docs. Cursor like commands are available in the co-pilot. The diagrams in the chapter reading pain are dynamic and interactive instead of static. They can see their class notes, AI organises it. They have a way to see transcribed notes from the class and action items/assignments. They can ask AI to generate a quiz inline to test their knowledge  - it is adaptive. There is a warm up section from previous chapters to reinforce long term memory.
- They can also see the concept mastery graph view to understand where they are, etc. This can be gamified. The system learns what the student is good and what it is not and uses this understanding to dynamically add new material or create a study guide. 
- Traditional exercises and courseworks are replaced by an Playground/Sandbox where they are put into a real world situtation or simulation where they are required to apply their knowledge, justify their decisions, and get real time feedback - the clues are progressive. The experience is adaoptive. AI copy paste does not work here and the AI copilot plays the role of a socratic teacher or adverserial guide. The sandbox enables them to test if the skills are tranferable.
